Project Title:Fitness App

About The Project:
1. Workout Screen: 
   - Features a calendar interface at the top, allowing users to track their workouts for the week.
   - Below the calendar is the "Today's Workout" section, highlighting exercises such as Jumping Jacks, Squats, and Bicycle Crunches, with the number of repetitions for each.

2. Login Screen:
   - Includes fields for email and password input, along with options for "Forgot Password?" and a "Sign Up" link for new users.
   - The background image of an individual exercising sets the app's tone as a fitness-focused application.
